Fixed an issue where the [[/LucasSimpsonsHitAndRunModLauncher/Hacks/CustomFiles/LuaFunctions/GetSetting.md]] and [[/LucasSimpsonsHitAndRunModLauncher/Hacks/CustomFiles/LuaFunctions/GetSettings.md]] functions read the low 32-bits of the 64-bit signed integer setting values as unsigned and casted that to a 64-bit signed integer to give it to Lua. This caused negative integer setting values to wrap as if they were 32-bit unsigned values.
